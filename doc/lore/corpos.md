This doc describes a fantasy world where mega corporations make giant battle mechs that compete for planetary domination. 

# Mech Manufacturers
The top Mega Corps that manufacture mechs are:
1. Helios Dominion Systems - A solar-energy empire that fields fast mechs loaded with energy weapons
2. Valkyr Forge Consortium - An industrial war syndicate known for supplying the world with deadly mercinaries. Specializes in mechs with high damage balistic weapons.
3. Nyx Meridian Dynamics - A tech giant that gained power through mech software development and computer engineering. Typically heavily shielded mechs with high amount of augments.
4. Obsidian Crown Armaments - A ruthless weapons conglomerate that treats planetary conquest as a shareholder growth strategy. Known for deploying quick mechs with high damage melee attacks
5. Iron Warden Foundries - A co-op backed by a workers union that builts nearly all the civil infrastructure of the planet. Manufactures slow mechs with high armor and long range missle attacks.

# Mech Structural Composition

Mechs are composed of each of these structural components. Each mech has one of each:

1. Head
2. Torso
3. Left Arm
4. Right Arm
5. Left Leg
6. Right Leg

Each of these structural components have a hull attribute. Once all of the hull points of a component are reduced to zero, that component is destoryed and the mech can no longer use that component or any of its attached augments. A mech is considered fully eliminated and non-functional once any of these conditions are met:

1. The head is destroyed
2. The torso is destroyed
3. Both legs are destroyed

If a single leg is lost, the mech can still function, although at 50% normal move speed.

Each structural component also has a wieght limit only allowing a maximum accumulated wieght for attached augments on that component.

# Mech Armor Types

There are various mech armor types. They are not exclusive as any mech can have any amount of each equiped however each has downsides to it

1. Energy Shield - causes extra heat build up for actions that generate heat
2. Armor Plating - weighs more


# Mech Weapons

Each weapon can be categorized into a main classification. Each with its own advantages and disadvantages:

1. Energy - high heat generation, deals extra damage to shields
2. Ballistic - low heat generation, requires reloading. Deals extra damage to armor.
3. Missile - low heat generation, fires in multiples. Each missle counts as a separate attack
4. Melee - no heat generation, high damage, requires mech to be adjacent to target

# Augments

Each structural componenet can have any number of augment slots that allows aditional weapons or auxillary augments to be attached.
Each augment falls under one category:

- External Mounted Weapon
- In-Frame weapon
- Hand Held weapon
- Armor
- Pilot Ability
- Mech Ability

Each augment is assigned a wieght attribute. The total combined weight of augements assigned to a structural component cannot exeed the weight limit of that component.
